About Us

About BE BETTER BSBL

Founded by Jake Fine, a blue-collar RV worker and lifelong baseball fan, BE BETTER BSBL started as an idea in his basement to create motivational baseball apparel. What began as a simple concept has evolved into something much bigger: a movement built around baseball culture and youth development.

Our vision goes beyond clothing. BE BETTER BSBL is laying the groundwork for a state-of-the-art baseball performance complex where athletes can train, compete, and grow. Every supporter, every purchase, and every rep taken in our gear helps us build that future.

"You can always be better, on the field and in life. That's not just our motto—it's our way of life."

Jake Fine Founder, BE BETTER BSBL
