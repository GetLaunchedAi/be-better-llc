About Us

About BE BETTER BSBL

BE BETTER BSBL is more than a baseball apparel brand—it's a mentality. Founded by Jake Fine, a blue-collar RV worker and lifelong baseball fan, the brand was built on one belief: you can always be better, on the field and in life.

What started in my basement as an idea to create motivational apparel has evolved into something much bigger: a movement built around baseball culture, youth development, and the relentless pursuit of being better every single day. Every piece of apparel carries more than a logo—it's a reminder to dig deeper, play with pride, and live the Be Better mentality.

"Our vision goes beyond clothing. BE BETTER BSBL is laying the groundwork for a state-of-the-art baseball performance complex where athletes can train, compete, and grow."

Jake Fine Founder, BE BETTER BSBL
